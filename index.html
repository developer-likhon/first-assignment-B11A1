renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

"Bangladesh is a beautiful country in South Asia, known for its lush green landscapes and rich cultural heritage. It is home to the world’s largest mangrove forest, the Sundarbans, where the majestic Royal Bengal Tiger resides. The people of Bangladesh are warm, hospitable, and proud of their traditions, including vibrant festivals like Pohela Boishakh. The country boasts an extensive river network, earning its title "Land of Rivers." Dhaka, the bustling capital city, showcases a blend of history and modernity. Bangladesh is also famed for its exquisite handicrafts, jute products, and garments. Despite challenges, it has shown remarkable resilience and progress in various sectors. The cuisine, featuring dishes like biryani and pithas, reflects its culinary diversity."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
